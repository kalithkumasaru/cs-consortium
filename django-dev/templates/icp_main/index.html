About

The NDSU Computer Science Industry/University Consortium Program(ICP) is dedicated to strengthening ties between NDSU and regional industry. The goal of the consortium is to perform basic and applied research designed to enhance the capabilities of its industrial and government affilities. These companies will work with the Computer Science Department to define research projects of interest to the companies. The results of this research will become avialable to memebr organizations.
