Research Projects

Sugar Beet Yield Prediction

The project starts by extracting geographical position information from log files that are stored on tractors and on trucks that collect the harvested sugarbeets and deliver them to a weight station. The output will be Shape Files containing definitions of spatial polygons together with the yield that was achieved from the respective spatial area. The work involves matching trucks to tractors based on the stored GPS locations, and identifying at what point the collection for a specific truck starts and ends. The project continues by taking current and historic satellite images and predicting yield on their basis, as well as relating image/yield data to grower information such as crop rotations.

Mobile Transportation Query System

Mobile devices are growing fast, and they provide great convenience to access information. This project will design a mobile transportation query system for iPhone, the most popular smartphone. Queries are sent to an oracle server from an iPhone. Then, relevant information is displayed on a 4'' screen with high usability. The proposed work allows federal agents to quickly retrieve information of a truck from anywhere at anytime.
